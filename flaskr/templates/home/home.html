Welcome to our application !

Here are the features available on our application. Please feel free to go through each of them.

Map

First tab allows user to enter an address and a radius. From the input, our app will find the address and print all the train stations in the area given.

Our app will find the address' latitude and longitude using Folium and Geopy Python libraries. From this, we will build a map with pins on the closest train stations in the radius given. On each pin is printed the name of the train station.

Train Schedules

Third tab aims to print all the upcoming trains in area defined by the user.

Given an address and limit of kilometers, our app will find all the upcoming trains which will departure from a train station in the perimeter calculated thanks to the limit inputed.

Search itinerary

This tab gives the option to search for an itinerary through a departure station and an arrival station.

Auto-completion has been implemented in order to help the user identify the different train stations and make sure he chooses an existing one..
